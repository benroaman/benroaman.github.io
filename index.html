trialSource g

trialSource is my Iron Yard final project. It is a platform for clinical trial recruitment designed to refine the candidate pool. As the project lead I was resposible for coordinating the efforts of the front and back end teams. I built the front end using Jquery, Lodash, and Rlite. I did not use a framework for this SPA as I felt the resulting challenges would force me to develop my skills and thinking as a programmer at large, not just as a front end dev.

Memory g

My version of the classic game memory, tuned for speed. A strong contender for my favorite Iron Yard project, this was our first extensive use of Jquery. As a lover of video games, I had a blast endlessly tweaking various aspects of the experience. Being a game this project also required the most in depth integration of DOM manipulation with background logic in the entire course. My best time was 19.58s on hard, Think you can beat that?

User Mgmt g

This is a simple asset management tool that allows you to save and manipulate some basic information about users/contacts. Another Iron Yard weekend project, this was an especially fun challenge as we had yet to touch Jquery, so I coded this app entirely with dry javascript. It was also our first exploration of how to really tie DOM manipulation to manipulation of data going on behind the scenes.
